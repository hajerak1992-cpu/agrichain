What is AgriChain?

AgriChain is a blockchain-powered platform that enables secure tracking of crops from farm to shelf. It ensures ethical practices, fair payments, and real-time traceability for all agricultural stakeholders.

Transparency

Track every step of the supply chain with immutable blockchain records.

Smart Contracts

Automated payments between farmers and buyers based on predefined terms.

Certifications

Store and verify organic, fair trade, and pesticide-free certifications.

How It Works

1. Farmers Upload Data

Crop origin and harvest info are recorded on the blockchain.

2. IoT Tracks Logistics

Sensors and QR codes track handling and transport.

3. Smart Contracts Pay Fairly

Payments are released directly based on verified delivery.
